// ABOUT US

What we do

We are a Kenyan-based social enterprise transforming agriculture and rural livelihoods through digital-innovation, digitizing VSLAs, connecting farmers to markets with the Agri-Trade platform, and delivering knowledge via NOVA BOX

Our MIS for value chains provides real-time data, enabling farmers, women, and youth to access finance, markets, and skills. Driving sustainable growth and resilience across Africa's agricultural sector.

// OUR

Mission

To empower farmers, women, and youth in Africa through inclusive digital solutions that intergrates finance, markets, and Knowledge - driving sustainable livelihoods, financial inlcusion, and community resilience.

// OUR FOCUS

Problem

Smallholder farmers and rural enterprenuers in Africa face fragmented supply chains,limited market access, low financial inclusion, and inadequate digital tools. Over 2 in 3 farmers lack access to digital marketplaces, while women-led groups and youth entreprenuers remain excluded from finance and scalable opprtunities.
