// ABOUT US

What we do

We are a Kenyan based social enterprise developing digital solutions for Agriculture, Health and WASH sectors in Kenya and Africa at large.

The company's core business revolves around agriculture, health and the WASH value chain working towards empowering the African enterprises in these sectors

// OUR FOCUS

Problem

Smallholder farmers and rural enterprenuers in Africa face fragmented supply chains,limited market access, low financial inclusion, and inadequate digital tools. Over 2 in 3 farmers lack access to digital marketplaces, while women-led groups and youth entreprenuers remain excluded from finance and scalable opprtunities.
