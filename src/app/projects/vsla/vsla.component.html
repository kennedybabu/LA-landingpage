// WHO WE ARE

What we do

Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLAs) are community-based financial groups where members pool savings and provide loans to each other. Traditionally managed with physical cash, lockboxes, and paper records.

Our digital VSLA platform transforms these groups with secure, transparent, and efficient digital financial management.

Members access their accounts via mobile phones, track transactions in real-time, and build formal credit histories that unlock access to banks, MFIs, and insurance products.

The platform integrates with mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money) and formal banking systems, creating a bridge between informal community finance and the formal financial sector.

// OUR FOCUS AREAS
  • Empowering smallholder farmers
  • Youth employability
  • Digital transformation for communities
  • Eliminate risks of theft, loss, or mismanagement of physical cash
  • Build formal credit histories that unlock bank loans and mortgages
  • Access larger loan amounts as groups demonstrate creditworthiness
  • Increase savings discipline through mobile money automation
  • Save time with automated calculations and digital records
  • Women gain financial independence with private digital accounts
