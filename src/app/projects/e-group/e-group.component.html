// WHO WE ARE

What we do

E-Group is a digital jobs and e-commerce platform designed specifically for rural youth and women who have been excluded from the digital economy.

The platform provides training, tools, and market access for freelancing, e-commerce, digital marketing, and online business opportunities.

Integrated with NOVA BOX digital libraries, E-Group ensures members can learn digital skills even without internet access, then apply those skills to earn income online.

By connecting rural talent with global opportunities, we're creating sustainable digital jobs that don't require migration to urban centers.
